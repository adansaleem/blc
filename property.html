Property/Land Laws and Construction Laws

The Firm provides best services on Land and Construction related matters. We offer our services for Due Diligence, Opinion, Drafting and registration of Land and documents, Property Tax matters, Litigation and presentation before different authorities etc. the Firm has a vast experience to work for LDA, Lahore Cantonment Board and other Local Bodies as well as various Companies and individuals.

Litigation Report

Represented Lahore Cantonment Board, before Honourable Lahore High Court Lahore, in cases regarding the levy of tax on transfer of immovable properties, situated within the territorial limit of a Cantonment Board.

Represented Lahore Cantonment Board Lahore, in constitutional petition, wherein the Petitioner has challenged the working of Executive officers of Cantonment Boards as Controller of Rents under Cantonment Rent Restriction Act 1963.

Represented Lahore Cantonment Board in Intra Court Appeal filed by the Punjab Agriculture and Meat Company, wherein PAMC has raised the issue of trade of commodities between the territorial jurisdictions of two distinct local authorities.

Represented Lahore Cantonment Board in various appeals filed before Director Military Lands and Cantonments.

Represented Sarghodha Cantonment Board in number of cases before Lahore High Court, Lahore.

Represented LDA in 2013 in Civil and High Courts.
