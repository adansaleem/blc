Energy Sector

The Firm is representing Power Companies before Courts to secure and recover their investments and funds, embezzlement matters by the employees or third party etc at Civil as well as criminal forum, handling the oil and gas and other regulatory matters, representations & communication with SNGPL & OGRA and litigation and energy-related environmental litigation.

Litigation Report

Challenged the constitutional validity of Gas Infrastructure Development Cess Ordinance 2014. The Firm has successfully obtained the stay order from honourable Lahore High Court Lahore, wherein, the court has restrained the officials of SNGPL from collecting PKR 2,292,627, 123/- as GIDC cess. The Firm is first law firm in Punjab who has challenged the constitutionality of the Ordinance and gotten the Ordinance suspended.

Representing Dong Fang Electric Corporation in Courts and obtained the restraining orders for encashment of Bank Guarantee from Civil Court Lahore which is still continued.

Defended BYCO Petroleum Pakistan Limited in criminal case lodged against its officials by an owner of Show Room burnt due to fire set at Petrol Station.
