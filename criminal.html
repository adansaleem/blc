Criminal Sector

The Firm provides best services to handle criminal matters and especially white collar crimes and recovery matters. The Firm assists its corporate clients throughout the criminal proceedings while presenting and defending started from FIRs, Bails and Criminal Trials. We present before Drug Courts, Magistrate Courts, Sessions Courts, FIA proceedings, NAB Proceedings etc.

Litigation Report

Assisted SSGC (LPG) for obtaining Red Warrants and arrest of the accused person through Interpol, Remand of the accused and sent him Jail.

Defended the personal of Abbott Laboratories Pakistan before Drug Courts in Criminal case and Represented Johnson & Johnson Pakistan Private Limited before Drugs Court at Faisalabad.

The Firm has been engaged by the Federal Investigation Agency as special prosecutor to prosecute cases regarding manufacturing of spurious and unregistered drugs.
