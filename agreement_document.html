Agreements & Contracts

Negotiating terms and finalization of agreements is like putting restraints on your fate. Lack of due care and proper legal advice may put destiny in the hands of others e.g. adjudicators. Our law firm realizes that covenants, terms and conditions agreed between the parties are the primary things upon which the different provisions of law are to be applied in courts. Our law firm provides consultancy at this stage of formation of contracts. We have been particular in observing the impact and effect of certain clauses of contracts, the application of law thereon and safeguarding the interests of our clients by taking safe sides under law..
