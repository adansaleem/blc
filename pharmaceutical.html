Pharmaceutical Sector

The Firm is handling the cases of pharmaceutical industry before High Courts, Drug Courts, and Consumer Courts and presenting the Copanies before DRAP, PCQB and other boards and authorities.

Handling litigation in Federal and Punjab Services Tribunals; Labour and Appellate Courts.

Litigation Report

Challenged the Import of Drugs Policy 2013, promulgated by Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan, before Sindh High Court at Karachi on behalf of Pakistan Chemist and Druggists Association.

Filed and pursued the constitutional petition before honorable Lahore High Court, Lahore, wherein, the composition/constitution of Punjab Quality Control Board under Punjab Drugs Rules, 2007 has been challenged by Pakistan Pharmaceutical Manufacturers Associations.

Provided recommendations and suggestions, on behalf of Pakistan Pharmaceutical Manufacturers Association and Pakistan Chemists and Druggists Association, to Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan regarding formulation of Drugs Pricing Policy Order 2014.

Challenged the vires of the Punjab Drugs (amendment) Ordinance, 2015 in writ petition No. titled as “Pakistan Pharmaceutical Manufacturers association and others vs Province of Punjab”. The Honourable Lahore High Court has passed interim relief in the said writ petition and restrained the Government of Punjab to take any coercive action against the pharmaceutical manufacturer without due process of law.

Filed and pursued the constitutional petitions on behalf of pharmaceutical companies and the PPMA regarding Toll Manufacturing Policy of Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan (DRAP), wherein, the ban on toll manufacturing of drugs had been challenged.

Advised various leading pharmaceutical companies of Pakistan regarding inquiry proceedings initiated by the National Accountability Bureau pursuant to allegations of obtaining illegal exorbitant increase in the maximum retail prices of several drugs, in connivance with officials of DRAP.

Represented Johnson & Johnson Pakistan Private Limited before Drugs Court at Faisalabad..

The Firm has been engaged by the Federal Investigation Agency as special prosecutor to prosecute cases regarding manufacturing of spurious and unregistered drugs.

Also being a retained counsel for individual pharmaceutical companies, The Firm has been advising them on a daily basis on the various legal issues including but not limited to challenging laboratory reports where a sample has been declared substandard, challenging show cause notices issued by DRAP, intellectual property, HR and other related matters.

Represented various pharmaceutical companies before Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan in cases and inquires pertaining to quality of their pharmaceutical products.

Filed various constitutional petitions on behalf of leading national and multinational Pharmaceutical companies regarding the pricing issues of their drugs.

Representing Various Companies in Criminal matters before Drug Courts and High Court filed against their personal as well as filed by the Companies against individuals.
